# Fiverr Gig Guide: High-End Landing Pages

This guide outlines exactly how to list your new sample portfolio piece on Fiverr to maximize your chances of getting orders. It's optimized for high value and professionalism.

## 1. Gig Titles (SEO Optimized)

Choose one of these titles based on your preferred angle. The bold words are your main keywords.

- **Option 1 (Recommended):** I will design a modern SaaS **landing page website**
- **Option 2:** I will build a high converting **responsive landing page**
- **Option 3:** I will create a premium **startup landing page** design
- **Option 4:** I will design and build a custom **HTML CSS landing page**

## 5. FAQ Section (Add to Fiverr)

A: I write custom HTML, CSS, and JS. This ensures your site is uniquely tailored, extremely fast, and free of unnecessary code.

**Q: Will the website be mobile-friendly?**
A: Absolutely! I use a mobile-first approach. Your landing page will look stunning on all device sizes.

**Q: Do you provide domain/hosting?**
A: I do not provide domain and hosting, but I provide the full source code files which can be uploaded to any web host (like Netlify, Vercel, or standard cPanel) easily.

## 6. Portfolio Presentation Tips

1. **Take Full-Page Screenshots:** Use an extension like "GoFullPage" to take a scrolling screenshot of the `index.html` page we built.
2. **Create Mockups:** Put your screenshot into a "Laptop Mockup" to make your main gig image look highly professional (you can use Canva or Figma for this).
3. **Use the "LaunchKit" brand:** The dummy product we built (LaunchKit) clearly demonstrates your ability to build software/tech landing pages, which are typically high-paying clients.
